## üí° Quick Tips

### Planning Your Visit
1. **Book 3 months in advance** - Mark your calendar for the first Friday
2. **Check day-specific sevas** - Archana only on Tue/Wed/Thu, Astadala only on Friday
3. **Set reminders** - Booking window is only 4 days (Friday to Tuesday)
4. **Have multiple options** - Some sevas are lottery-based

### Booking Process
1. Register at www.tirumala.org
2. Login during booking window (First Friday 10 AM - Following Tuesday 10 AM)
3. Select desired seva and date
4. Complete payment
5. Receive confirmation via email/SMS

### Important Notes
- Booking is done through online lottery system
- Current booking available at Single Window Counter, Tirumala (one day advance)
- For queries: Call 0877-2277777 or 0877-2233333 (24x7)
